The pentatonic scales


The pentatonic scales are scales of five notes.
In particular, they lack of degrees 4 ° and 7 ° can (for more fingering), while the 2 nd and 7 ° can (fingerings for minors). This feature makes them particularly used in different musical genres, such as: Jazz, Blues, Pop /Rock.
There are five basic pentatonic scales, fingering which is reported in chapters 8 and 9.
