Pentatonic scales


Pentatonic scales are scales that have the particularity to be composed of five notes. In particular, they lack of degrees 2 ° and 6 ° (for minor scales) and 4 ° and 7 ° (for major scales) . This makes them particularly usable in various genres, such as: Jazz, Blues, Pop / Rock.
