The Jazz Guitar

Jazz

Jazz is a genre of origin American born in the early twentieth century in the African American community southern United States from the confluence of African musical traditions and Europe. Distinguishing features of this kind are intensive use of improvisation, often syncopated swing rhythm and tone melancholy (as the use of blue notes). Jazz music is a folk music born out diverse cultures and peoples, which has expanded from America to Europe, up to reach the global spread. Jazz music is a discipline and total complex, based on improvisation, impromptu expression of its and the subconscious self, which melt. In Jazz, as in any field of music, the expression of improvisation, coincides with the attainment of nothingness and completely and this is developed by creating melodies new and instantly developed while the artist plays.
